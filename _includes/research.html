Research

My interests are broad, but encompass skills learned in the lab, the greenhouse, and at the terminal. I believe in open science and a commitment to code sharing.



Sex Chromosomes

I am using a suite of reverse genetic, sequencing, and bioinformatic strategies to identify gender determination genes on the garden asparagus Y chromosome

Polyploidy

Whole genome duplications have run amok in the flowering plants. I enjoy taking phylogenomic approaches with genome and transcriptome data to identify these events in big trees.

Basal Angiosperms

The basal angiosperms are a particularly special group of angiosperms. I am especially interested in Amborella trichopoda, the most basal extant angiosperm. And it's dioecious, too!
